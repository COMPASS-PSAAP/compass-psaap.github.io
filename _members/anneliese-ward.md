Anneliese Ward is the University of New Mexico grant manager for the PSAAP IV COMPASS. She is the Sr. Business Manager at the Center for Advanced Research Computing at UNM, in addition to serving on the UNM Staff Council and on the UNM Women in Tech board. She received her Master's in Business Administration in 2021 and her Master's in Cybersecurity and Business Analytics in 2024.
